Overview: The Power Supply Unit

The power supply unit is what feeds the computer and its associated components with electricity. The power supply unit will often have contingencies in place so that, in the case of a power surge, the capacitators will often burn up in place of the components. In the case of a power failure, the power supply unit would often depower the computer in a manner that is not harmful to its components. The 80Plus rating of a power supply is an official rating regarding the efficacy of the unit and its safety.

To filter below the options, type in the wattage of interest or select the 80 Plus rating of interest. Alternatively you can choose to visualize the brands.
